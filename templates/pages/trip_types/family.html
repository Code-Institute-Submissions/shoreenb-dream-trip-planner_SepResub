Santorini offers a combination of picturesque white-washed architecture, idyllic volcanic beaches and delightful hilltop hamlets. But as much as the island will entice you with its beauty and charm, the list of things to do will surpass every expectation.

Thailand

Tantalising Thailand is a gem for exploration. From the jungles and mountains of the north, to the utopian beaches and castaway islands of the south. Discover captivating wildlife, paradise beaches created for the perfect holiday and inspiring culture. Visit spiritual temples, meet humble and happy locals and wander as many street markets as you can.

Orlando

Visit Orlando – a hub bursting with ultimate experiences for thrill-seekers and Disney-loving Instagrammers. Get lost in Orlando’s premium outlet malls, visit the Kennedy Space Centre or take a drive out to the beaches.
